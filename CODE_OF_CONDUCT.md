# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We want AegisForge to be a respectful, technically serious, and collaborative project.

## Expected behavior

- be respectful and constructive
- assume good faith
- focus criticism on ideas and implementation, not people
- help keep discussions clear and grounded

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment or intimidation
- personal attacks
- discriminatory language
- deliberate disruption of collaboration

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to project discussions, issues, pull requests, and related collaboration spaces.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when behavior clearly harms the project or its contributors.
